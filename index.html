What's inside

Four tools, one window,
zero tab-switching.

Everything we built on top of the Google + HubSpot + Gong triangle so your mornings don't start with twelve browser tabs.

01 · Automation

Gong Scraper

Pulls call transcript links straight out of Gong, matches them to the right company, and drops them into a per-company Drive folder — fully background-queued with live progress. Your Gong session persists between opens, so you log in once and forget about it.

gong_collect drive_organize auto-retry
02 · Personalization

Draggable Dashboard

Every widget — stats, upcoming calls, recent jobs, knowledge search — is drag-to-rearrange. Hide the ones you don't use; they're tucked into Add Table and ready whenever you change your mind. Layout is saved per-user to local storage.

drag · drop hideable persistent
03 · Search

Knowledge Base + Assistant

A fuzzy in-memory search (Fuse.js) over internal and customer-facing wiki content — right from the dashboard widget. Click a result to open it, or jump to the Assistant for a broader browse. Rebuild the index whenever the source docs change.

Fuse.js internal + customer rebuildable
04 · Tooling

Scrub & Split CSV

Drop in a messy export — dedupe rows, split columns, and normalize in seconds. Pair it with the HubSpot import and a scheduled cron job (hourly, weekdays-only, whatever) and the Book of Business stays clean on its own.

CSV · dedupe HubSpot import cron schedules
